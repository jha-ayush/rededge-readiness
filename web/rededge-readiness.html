How it works

What this is

A pre-flight check for the MicaSense RedEdge and Altum multispectral cameras. It reads the camera over its own WiFi and tells you, in one glance, whether the sensor is ready to capture usable data. It reports sensor readiness, not airspace or flight legality.

Reading the result

GOEvery monitored system is within tolerance. Clear to capture.
CHECKSomething needs attention, or could not be confirmed. Review before flying.
NO-GOA blocking problem, or no link to the camera. Do not fly until resolved.

Fail toward caution: anything unconfirmed is treated as CHECK; a lost link is NO-GO. The tool never shows a clear pass on missing data.

What each check verifies

SD storageCard present, writable, and above your free-space floor.
GPS fixEnough satellites for reliable geotagging, no interference.
Position accuracyReported error within your maximum.
Light sensorDLS irradiance sensor active, needed for reflectance work.
Supply voltageCamera supply above your floor.
Time sourceValid clock for timestamps.
Camera rigExpected cameras online, firmware consistent.
FirmwareMatches the version you expect, if set.

Thresholds are yours to set. Open Settings to adjust the camera address and every limit.
